Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students
- Always check the sender’s email address before clicking on any link or downloading attachments
- Do not share your passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone, even if the message looks official
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links, especially those that create urgency
- Enable two factor authentication on important accounts like email, banking, and social media
- Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms

  
UPI and Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
- Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money through UPI apps
- Do not approve “collect requests” from unknown people on apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, or Paytm

General Safety

- Keep your apps and devices updated to avoid security issues
- Be careful while using public WiFi for banking or sensitive activities

In case of cyber fraud

- Immediately report the issue on the National Cyber Crime Portal cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the helpline number 1930 as soon as possible for quick assistance
