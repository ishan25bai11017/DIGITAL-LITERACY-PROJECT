Social Media Use Checklist for College Students

Do’s

- Think before posting anything online
- Keep your profile information accurate and professional
- Respect others and communicate politely
- Check the authenticity of links and messages before clicking
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content

Don’ts

- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Do not post misleading or false content
- Do not engage in online arguments or negative behavior
- Do not upload content that could harm your reputation
- Do not trust unknown links or messages without verification
